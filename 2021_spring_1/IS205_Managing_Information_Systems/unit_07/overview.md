# Unit 7: Overview  

Introduction

Chapter 13

Shares with us that during your career, you will undoubtability be asked to work on the development of a new system to solve an important challenge for the firm. In the process of building new systems, you will face many choices about hardware and software, and you will have to think about how to redesign business processes and jobs in order to maximize the value derived from the new system. You will also have to decide whether to build your own software solution, or purchase a packaged software or online Web services. But the most important decisions you will face involve understanding just exactly what it is you want the software to do and what the value it will bring to your firm.

Chapter 14

Imparts that during your career you will undoubtably work on information system projects to solve an important challenge for you firm. Whether the project entails building a new information system or enhancing an existing system., you will need to know how to measure the business benefits of these investments, and how to make sure that these systems work successfully in your organization. The success of your project will depend on how well it is managed.

Unit Learning Objectives

After you complete this unit you should be able to:

Chapter 13

Demonstrate how building new systems produces organizational change.

Identify and describe the core activities in the systems development process.

Evaluate alternative methods for building information systems.

Compare alternative methodologies for modeling systems.

Identify and describe new approaches for system-building in the digital firm era.

Chapter 14

Identify and describe the objectives of project management and why it is so essential in developing information systems.

Compare models for selecting and evaluating information systems projects and methods for aligning IS projects with the firm’s business goals.

Evaluate models for assessing the business value of information systems.

Analyze the principle risk factors in information systems projects.

Select appropriate strategies for managing project risk and system implementation.
