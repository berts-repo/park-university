# Unit 7: Solving Quadratic Equations

Introduction

In this section, you will learn to solve quadratic equations, which model the cross sections of telescopes, satellite dishes, and automobile headlights. In addition bridge support cables are modeled by quadratic equations.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Solve equations using the square root property. (CLO 4)

Solve quadratic equations by completing the square. (CLO 4 and 9)

Solve quadratic equations using the quadratic formula. (CLO 4 and 10)

Determine the number and type of solutions to a quadratic equation using the discriminant. (CLO 1)
