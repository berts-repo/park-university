# Notice of Required Remote Proctoring for Online Students

Online & Pirate Patch students please note:

This course (ONLINE & Pirate Patch Sections ONLY) requires that you take the final exam while being remotely monitored by an electronic proctor via ProctorU.

Face-to-Face or Blended sections of this course will NOT use ProctorU.

There will be no charge to you for this proctoring if you schedule your session in advance here:  [link: https://go.proctoru.com/] https://go.proctoru.com/ (Links to an external site.)Links to an external site, or click the "ProctorU" menu link on the left to schedule your exam.

Complete details on set-up, privacy issues and scheduling your ProctorU final-exam session can be found on the Park Distance Learning - Academic Policies -  Park University Online Proctoring Policy Page here, URL:  [link: https://www.park.edu/academics/park-distance-learning/academic-policies/] https://www.park.edu/academics/park-distance-learning/academic-policies/ . Please review the Online Proctoring Policy Page and its related documentation & instructions carefully.

Students needing testing accommodation for a qualifying disability must follow the instructions in the Park University Online Proctoring Policy Page (see URL above).

For any additional issues related to online proctoring of this course, contact your course instructor.
