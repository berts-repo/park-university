# Unit 5: Rational Expressions and Functions

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn about rational expressions including how to simplify them and how to perform arithmetic operations involving them.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Write rational expressions in lowest terms. (CLO 6)

Multiply and divide rational expressions. (CLO 6)

Add and subtract rational expressions. (CLO 6)

Simplify complex fractions. (CLO 6)
