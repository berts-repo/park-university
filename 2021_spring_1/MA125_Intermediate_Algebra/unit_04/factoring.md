# Unit 4: Factoring

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn how to factor polynomials. One application of this technique is determining the maximum area that can be enclosed by a fence of fixed length.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Factor out the greatest common factor from a polynomial. (CLO 4)

Factor polynomials by grouping. (CLO 4)

Factor trinomials. (CLO 4)

Factor special types of polynomials. (CLO 4)

Solve polynomial equations using the zero-factor property. (CLO 4)
