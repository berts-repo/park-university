# Unit 6: Roots, Radicals, and Root Functions

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn about square roots and radicals. One of the applications of the square root is the calculating the distance from the horizon to the top of a tall building or mountain.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Evaluate expressions with rational exponents or radicals. (CLO 3 and 7)

Convert between rational exponents and radicals. (CLO 7)

Simplify radicals. (CLO 7)

Add and subtract radicals. (CLO 7)

Multiply and Divide radicals. (CLO 7)

Solve equations with radicals. (CLO 8)
