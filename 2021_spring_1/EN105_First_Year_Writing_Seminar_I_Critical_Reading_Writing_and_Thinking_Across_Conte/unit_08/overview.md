# Unit 8: Overview

Style, Clarity, & Economy

Introduction

Welcome to the final unit of EN 105! In this unit, you will "zoom out" from this course to reflect on our values as writers, prepare for future writing tasks in and out of college classes, and practice editing your prose for style, clarity, and economy. By the end of this unit, you will have completed your final Writer's Journal entry, engage in discussion, and complete a brief editing quiz. You will also, by the end of this unit, receive instructor feedback and your grade on your Writing Portfolio.

Good luck as you finish up your EN105 course!

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of this unit, you will be able to:

Reflect explicitly on your writing process (CLO 1, 2)

Edit your prose for style, clarity, and economy (CLO 1, 4, 5)

Use common academic documentation guidelines for writing (CLO 5)
