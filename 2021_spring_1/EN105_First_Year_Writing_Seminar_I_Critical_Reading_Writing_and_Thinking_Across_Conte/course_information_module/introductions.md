# Introductions

Welcome! Let's get to know each other...

Hello, and welcome again to English 105: First Year Writing Seminar I: Critical Reading, Writing, and Thinking Across Contexts. The course title is a little long, but it describes the three major activities you will participate in this term: reading, writing, and thinking. Before you start our hard work, use this space to get to know your classmates and instructor.

Instructions

As soon as you can, please take a few moments to post a short introduction, using the instructions below. After you have posted your introduction, check back often to learn about your classmates! In order to understand how to use the discussion board, try to respond to at least two posts made by your classmates or instructor.

Please craft a paragraph or two that describes the following:

Your name, location, and intended major or program of study

Your career goals

Your extracurricular activities: what do you do besides attend college?

Your reasons for being in this class, and what you would like to take away from your experience this term

One writing strength: when it comes to writing, what do you feel confident about?

One writing challenge: when it comes to writing, what gives you trouble? What are your struggles?

Any other relevant information that your classmates should know about you
