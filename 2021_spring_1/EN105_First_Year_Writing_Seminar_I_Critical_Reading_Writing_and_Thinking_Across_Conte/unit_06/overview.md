# Unit 6: Overview

So What? Who Cares? Making Your Writing Matter

Introduction

Welcome to the sixth unit of EN105! In this unit, you will return to one of your major writing assignments and perform a substantial revision, using the responses of your classmates and the rhetorical concept of kairos to revise your assignment, making sure that your writing matters.

In this unit, you will focus on peer review and revision. Similar to unit 4, you will complete a Peer Review assignment and use your classmates' feedback to guide your work on your second Revision Project. As in unit 4, you will trade your drafts with an assigned peer review group early in the unit. It is imperative that you post your draft for your peer review group as soon as possible. Specific instructions can be found in the Peer Review assignment. 

Note: there is no reading quiz for this unit. However, your reading will help you to complete your Discussion assignment for this unit.

Your work in this unit will prepare you to do an excellent job on the course portfolio, which is the ultimate goal of the course. Good luck, and happy revising!

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of this unit, you will be able to:

Define and identify the rhetorical concept of kairos (CLO 3, 4)

Revise your writing to ensure that it matters to readers (CLO 1, 3, 4)

Use peer review as a writing process (CLO 1)
