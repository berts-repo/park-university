# Unit 7: Overview

Revising for Structure & Reflecting on your Learning

Introduction

Welcome to the seventh unit of EN105! This unit is a culmination of all the work you have done so far this term. In this unit, you will put together your Writing Portfolio, which will include further revision as well as reflection on your learning so far in the course.

For this unit's Peer Review, you will need to create a rough draft of your reflective essay for the portfolio. Be sure to re-read the portfolio instructions for a reminder of expectations and possible approaches for the reflective essay. Keep in mind that you will have a little more time to post your reflective essay draft this unit, and you won't be assigned a peer review group. Instead, it will be up to you to select a classmate whose reflective essay draft you will work with.

You should also use your time this unit to continue revising the two pieces you will include in your portfolio. Even if you select the two pieces you have revised in the Unit 4 and Unit 6 Revision Projects, your instructor will expect to see further revisions and edits to your work.

This unit is the culmination of all of your work so far -- good luck!

 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of this unit, you will be able to:

Use peer review as a writing process strategy (CLO 1)

Reflect explicitly on your learning in this course (CLO 1, 2)

Write with focus and clarity for a variety of purposes and genres (CLO 2)

Revise your writing by using diverse rhetorical strategies (CLO 3, 4)

Use common academic documentation guidelines for writing (CLO 5)
