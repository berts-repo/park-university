Well done on your assignment. The DHCP DISCOVERY message is sent as a broadcast.  This is something that you need to keep in mind when designing your DHCP configuration.  For example the broadcast do not pass through a router so if you have a DHCP server on one segment but your DHCP clients on a different segment, the DHCP DISCOVERY broadcasts will not reach the DHCP server simply because the broadcasts cannot pass through the router.
