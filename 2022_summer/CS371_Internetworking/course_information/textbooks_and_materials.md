Required Software:

Access to Cisco Networking Academy website:  [link: http://www.netacad.com] netacad.com.

Download and install the latest version of Cisco Packet Tracer software if you plan to use it in your local device.

Cisco Packet Tracer, obtainable through Cisco Networking Academy, can run on a Windows, Linux (Ubuntu),  Mac, or mobile device.

Detailed information about Cisco Packet Tracer can be found under Modules > Course Information > FAQ Packet Tracer.

Taking care of any issue regarding running the Cisco Packet Tracer software in your local device as early as possible in unit 1 is very important.

Access to Netlab ( [link: http://labs.fhsu.edu] labs.fhsu.edu).

Taking care of any issue regarding running Netlab in your local device as early as possible in unit 1 is very important.
