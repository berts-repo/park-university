As far as the broadcast domains, every VLAN is its own broadcast domain.  Since a switch has one VLAN, that means that the switch by default has 1 broadcast domain.  On the other hand, If you have a regular network segment, that is also its own broadcast domain.   So for example you have one router and two network segments are attached to it then you have 2 broadcast domains.   Broadcasts do not pass through a router.
