## Instructions

Introduction

This skills exam evaluates your hands-on computer networking skills using Cisco Packet Tracer.

Directions 

The exam is located at the Cisco Network Academy.

It has a title of SRWE Final Skills Exam (PTSA). See Figure 1 for its location.

Figure 1. The location of the PT Skills Assessment

The exam has a similar nature as a unit Cisco Packet Tracer project. That is, there will be a project specification and you will perform tasks to satisfy the project specification.

During the assigned unit, you can access the exam multiple times, and save your work by clicking “save” button. You can also submit the exam multiple times. The grade will post in the grade book with the last attempt that the student obtains in the exam.

Detailed information about Cisco Packet Tracer software, requirements, and guidelines can be found under Modules > “Course Information” > [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/68420/pages/faq-packet-tracer-netlab] FAQ: Packet Tracer, Netlab.

Rubric

Depending on the number of tasks that are required to complete in the exam, Cisco Packet Tracer will report the percentage of successful completion. For example, if there are 8 required tasks, and a student completes 6 tasks successfully, then it reports 6/8 (75%) completion.
