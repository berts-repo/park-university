## Instructions

Introduction

Before you take the Cisco final exam in unit 8, it is good to practice what you learn in this course through the Practice Final Exam - SRWE.  

Directions 

The exam is located at the Cisco Network Academy.

It has a title of Practice Final Exam - SRWE, see Figure 1.

Figure 1. Practice Final Exam - SRWE

The scope of the exam is modules 1-17.

Each exam consists of approximately 50 to 60 multiple-choice, multiple-answer, fill-in-the-blank, and matching questions.

You can take the exam 3 times, and the gradebook will record your score of the last attempt.

It has a time limit of 2 hours each time. 

Some questions in the final exam may need to run the Cisco Packet Tracer. Therefore, be sure to take the exam in the environment installed with Cisco Packet Tracer software.

Once you take the exam and click the submit button, Cisco automatically grades your exam and logs the exam result in your gradebook in Cisco Networking Academy. After the deadline, your instructor will transfer the score to your gradebook under Park Canvas site.
