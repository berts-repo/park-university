# Unit 8: Feedback

There are 2 kinds of Feedback in this course

Park University Student Opinion of Teaching Survey: Students are encouraged to take the survey. If a student completes and submits the affirmation of having taken the survey by the deadline, he or she will receive full credit for this survey.

Unit 8 Course Feedback discussion thread: This is optional, thus will not be graded. However, the instructor appreciates your feedback from your experience in going through the entire course.

Please discuss the following about the course

What did you find most challenging?

What did you find most interesting?

What did you find that was practical and could be applied in your work or school today?
