Unit 8: Final

Unit Learning Outcomes

In this course, you have learned about switching, routing, and wireless essentials. During this unit, you will take two final exams, and perform a Netlab skills assessment.

CLO#1. Differentiate various LAN, VLAN, WLAN technologies and designs.

CLO#2. Contrast different routing and switching mechanisms.

CLO#3. Produce router and switch configurations.

CLO#4. Select technologies to enhance networking security, reliability, availability, and performance.
