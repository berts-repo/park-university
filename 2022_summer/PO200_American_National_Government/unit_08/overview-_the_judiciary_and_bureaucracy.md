# Unit 8: Overview- The Judiciary and Bureaucracy

The Judiciary and Bureaucracy- Introduction

We’ll wrap up our discussion of the three branches with the judiciary, then we’ll move to an area often unseen but still important – the bureaucracy. In this unit, you’ll complete a reading quiz and a discussion board, as well as the second short paper assignment and the second exam.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Outline the structure of the federal court system (CLO 1)

Summarize the roles and duties of the judicial branch (CLO 1)

Identify the role of bureaucrats in policy implementation (CLO 4)

Summarize checks and balances for the Judiciary (CLO 1)
