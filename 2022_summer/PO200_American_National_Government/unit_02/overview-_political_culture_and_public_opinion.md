# Unit 2: Overview- Political Culture and Public Opinion

Political Culture and Public Opinion- Introduction

In Unit 2, we will discuss two distinct, but related, topics. We will study political culture – the norms and values we share as Americans and how our political culture differs from cultures in other nations. We will also study public opinion – looking at where various groups of citizens agree and disagree on questions of politics and public policy. You will complete a reading quiz and a discussion board for this unit.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

List the main elements of the American political view (CLO 1)

Contrast views on various issues between various ethnic, racial, and gender groups (CLO 2, CLO3)

Compare American public opinion with that of other Western democracies (CLO 2)
