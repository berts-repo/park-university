# Unit 3: Overview- Media

Media- Introduction

In Unit 3, we will study the relationship between media and politics. How do politicians try to use the media? How do media outlets try to use politicians? And what has been the changing nature of media as it relates to politics from the founding of our republic to the present? You will complete a reading quiz and a discussion board for this unit, as well as a short paper.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Outline the history of media in the United States (CLO 2)

Identify the ways in which politicians use the media and vice versa (CLO 2)

Interpret potential evidence of media bias (CLO 2)

List the rolls of the national media (CLO 2)
