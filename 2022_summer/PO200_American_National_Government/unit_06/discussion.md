Federalism in the United States is the combined political system of the states and the national government. The states can further be divided into subdivisions including county, cities and districts. 

I believe the there is to much power in the hands of national government, and even to much in the hands of the state in certain circumstances. The primary reason I believe this is the needs of the community are locally based. The needs and wants of people in a small town are going to be completely different from the needs of the community in New York. Having the power distributed also allows for a larger diversity of communities, as these different communities can gather around other like minded people and provided the needs of that particular group. If the government was even more centralized and all the power landed in just a few places, it would leave less checks and balances for someone with bad intentions to sneak into position.
