# Unit 6: Overview- Constitutional Structure and Federalism

Constitutional Structure and Federalism- Introduction

In this unit, we’ll step back in time and take a look at the Founding Fathers. What were their concerns in constructing a new form of government? Then, we’ll look at how their work takes hold in modern times, through our constitutional structure and federalism. You’ll complete a discussion board and a reading quiz during this unit.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the development of the Constitution (CLO 1)

Define federalism (CLO 1)
