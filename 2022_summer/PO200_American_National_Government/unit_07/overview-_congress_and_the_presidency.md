# Unit 7: Overview- Congress and the Presidency

Congress and the Presidency- Introduction

In this unit, we move on to the three branches of government, starting with the legislative and moving to the executive. What are the powers of each? How can they keep an eye on each other? In this unit you’ll complete a reading quiz and a discussion board.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Outline how a bill becomes a law (CLO 4)

Contrast the structure of the House of Representatives and the Senate (CLO 1)

Summarize checks and balances for Congress and the Presidency (CLO 1)
