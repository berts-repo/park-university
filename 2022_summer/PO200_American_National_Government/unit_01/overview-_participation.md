# Unit 1: Overview- Participation

Participation- Introduction

In Unit 1, we will learn about various forms of participation in politics. We will discuss
who participates, who doesn’t participate, and why, as well as the barriers various groups have faced to voting over time. You will complete a reading quiz and a discussion board for this unit.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Describe the various ways in which citizens can participate in politics (CLO 3)

Explain the factors which influence participation in politics (CLO 3)

Summarize the role of money in politics (CLO 3)
