# Unit 5: Overview- Political Parties and Interest Groups

Introduction

In Unit 5, we will discuss organizational units in American politics, specifically political parties and interest groups. We’ll trace the history of political parties, look at some different types of political powers, and see how interest groups use their power. You’ll complete a discussion board and a reading quiz as well as the first exam, covering Units 1-5.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Sketch the development of political parties in the United States (CLO 1)

Summarize the existence of third parties in the United States (CLO 1)

Explain the role and power of interest groups in politics (CLO 2)
