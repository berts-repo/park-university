# Unit 4: Overview- Voting and Elections

Voting and Elections- Introduction

In Unit 4, we will examine voting and elections, studying campaigns from the choice to run to the final outcome, and looking at voting and elections from the side of the candidates and the side of the voters. You will complete a reading quiz and a discussion board for this unit.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Describe the electoral process for Presidential and Congressional elections (CLO 3)

Analyze the role of advertising in elections (CLO 3)

Outline the factors that determine who wins and loses elections (CLO 3)

Recognize the relationship between elections and policy change (CLO 4)
