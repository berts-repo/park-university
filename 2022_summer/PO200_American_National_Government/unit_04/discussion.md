I believe the the role of advertising in politics is only going to increase, as platforms like Tiktok and Facebook continue to pull large user numbers. Having access to video sites, message boards, and instant news has made it easier for candidates quickly respond with messages and slogans to address the narrative of new and current events. Issues and policies regarding a current events are a central concern, and politicians take a position on these events to increase their chances at election. I personally would like to see less advertainment in politics, especially attack ads,  but I also don't see it as practical. If attack adds didn't exist it would be harder to discredit false information provided by a particular candidate. So, as not enjoying seeing candidates just creating negative slogans, I also find it important to keep information as correct as possible.
