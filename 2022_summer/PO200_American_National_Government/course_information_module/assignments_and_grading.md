Core Assessment

The core assessment consists of two examinations, one after Unit 5, and one after Unit 8. Each examination consists of multiple choice questions designed to test the student’s knowledge and recall of CLOs and ULOs, as well as short answer questions designed to test the students comprehension and critical thinking with respect to CLOs and ULOs. The total will amount to 400 out of 1000 points, or 40% of the grade, 20% for each exam.
