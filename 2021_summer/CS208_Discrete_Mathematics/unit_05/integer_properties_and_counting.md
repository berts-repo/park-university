# Unit 5: Integer Properties and Counting

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Integer Properties and Counting.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Identify integer properties. (CLO 4)

Examine division and modular algorithms. (CLO 4)

Examine various counting methods. (CLO 1 and 4)

Practice using various counting methods. (CLO 1 and 4)
