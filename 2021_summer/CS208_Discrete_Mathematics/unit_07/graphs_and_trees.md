# Unit 7: Graphs and Trees

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Graphs and Trees.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Construct graphs. (CLO 2)

Analyze graphs and their representations. (CLO 2)

Examine paths and circuits. (CLO 2)

Identify the properties of trees. (CLO 3)

Examine tree traversals and spanning trees. (CLO 3)
