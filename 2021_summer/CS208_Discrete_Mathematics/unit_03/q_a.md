# Unit 3: Q & A

The Q & A area is a non-graded area and is for students to communicate. This is your place to ask specific or technical questions about the concepts and topics in this unit. The instructor "may" post answers here occasionally. However, the instructor will be very active in the main course discussions.

Think about this area as your class study group. The more everyone participates, the more help everyone gets. The Information Technology community is very much a part of sharing and helping bring everyone to new levels of knowledge and skills. Use this to help share, as well as to get to know each other better in each unit. 
 
 Technologies are changing overnight. No one knows everything, or even most of what's out there. Learn to use resources wisely.

REMEMBER that Q & A is a discussion area, not a complaint area! It's for technical help in the course. If your question is of personal nature, please send the instructor an email via the Email tab at the top of the course frame.

Please post any question that you may have during this unit here.
