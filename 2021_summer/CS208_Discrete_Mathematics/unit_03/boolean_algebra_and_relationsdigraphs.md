# Unit 3: Boolean Algebra and Relations/Digraphs

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Boolean Algebra and Relations/Digraphs.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Examine Boolean algebra. (CLO 1)

Identify gates and circuits. (CLO 1)

Examine properties of relations. (CLO 1 and 2)

Construct directed graphs, paths and cycles. (CLO 1 and 2)
