# Unit 1: Logics and Sets

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Logic and Sets.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Examine propositions and logical operations. (CLO 1)

Evaluate compound propositions and conditional statements. (CLO 1)

Analyze logical equivalence. (CLO 1)

Identify predicates, quantifiers, and quantified statements. (CLO 1)

Examine sets and subsets. (CLO 1)

Inspect various set operations. (CLO 1)
