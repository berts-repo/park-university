How to sign up for a zyBook account:

To see how to sign up for zyBooks, please click the link:  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61678/files/8094218/download?verifier=vv9WtZ0VkM6eux1SABHlS5jQEtPo2AN1hSPRVqof&wrap=1] How to sign up for zyBooks.pdf.

Here are the steps:

Obtain a zyBook code from your instructor. If you do not receive a zyBook code from your instructor one week before the course starts, contact your instructor to get the code.

Go to  [link: http://www.zybooks.com] www.zybooks.com

Sign up.

Copy/paste the zyBook code where it belongs. The student will then be able to subscribe to his/her zyBook using a credit card or PayPal.

Notes:

It is free to access to Chapter 1 zyBook.

From zyBook publisher: If you need additional access beyond Chapter 1 (such as waiting for financial aid approval, GI Bill stipend, and so on), you can email  [link: mailto:support@zyBooks.com] support@zyBooks.com and ask for between 2 to 4 week deferral.  In this way, student activities in the zyBook will be recorded and the professor will be able to monitor their activity, just like the other students.

From Park University bookstore: If any students have concerns about the book, please send them to  [link: mailto:onlinestudents@park.edu] onlinestudents@park.edu and we can work with them. 

Each student needs his own zyBook account as the homework is to be done individually via his own zyBook account.
