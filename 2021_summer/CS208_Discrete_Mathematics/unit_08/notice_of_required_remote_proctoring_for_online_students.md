# Notice of Required Remote Proctoring for Online Students

Online & Pirate Patch students please note:

This course (ONLINE & Pirate Patch Sections ONLY) requires that you take the final exam while being remotely monitored by an electronic proctor via ProctorU.

Face-to-Face or Blended sections of this course will NOT use ProctorU.

There will be no charge to you for this proctoring if you schedule your session in advance here:  [link: https://go.proctoru.com/] https://go.proctoru.com/, or click the "ProctorU" menu link on the left to schedule your exam.

Complete details on set-up, privacy issues and scheduling your ProctorU final-exam session can be found on the  [link: https://www.park.edu/academics/park-distance-learning/academic-policies/#ProctorPolicy] Park Distance Learning - Academic Policies -  Park University Online Proctoring Policy Page  Please review the Online Proctoring Policy Page and its related documentation & instructions carefully.

Students needing testing accommodation for a qualifying disability must follow the instructions in the Park University Online Proctoring Policy Page (see URL above).

For any additional issues related to online proctoring of this course, contact your course instructor.

Important Technical Requirement

You will need to make sure the ProctorU extension is installed on Chrome or Firefox. At the time of your appointment, to connect with a proctor you will also need to install a temporary applet to allow the proctor to share their screen.  

The extension will only need to be installed once and can be done ahead of time, but the applet is a temporary file that must be installed for each ProctorU session.

You should visit the  [link: https://www.proctoru.com/live-plus-resource-center] ProctorU Live+ Resource Center, which provides videos, a list of what is needed, along with links to the downloads for those extensions as well.

ProctorU encourages you to visit the [link: https://test-it-out.proctoru.com/]  "Test It Out page" a few days before your exam, at about the same time of day as your scheduled exam, to ensure you have the best possible testing experience. You will be able to test your equipment and connect with a ProctorU representative for tech support 24/7 if needed.
