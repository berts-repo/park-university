Feedback

There are 2 kinds of Feedback in this course

Park University Student Opinion of Teaching Survey: Students are not required to fill out the teaching survey. However, an instructor appreciates your feedback on the course. The feedback is anonymous, and is worth 0.5% of your final grade.

Unit 8 Course Feedback discussion thread: This is optional, thus will not be graded. However, the instructor appreciates your feedback from your experience in going through the entire course.

Instructions

Please discuss the following about the course

What did you find most challenging?

What did you find most interesting?

What did you find that was practical and could be applied in your work or school or daily life?

It’s been a pleasure working with all of you! Best to your future endeavor in the field of Computer Science or Mathematics.
