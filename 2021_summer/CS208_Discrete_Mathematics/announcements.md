Class,

As we begin Unit 2 of our class - I would ask that you review this announcement on Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's) FIRST before sending any questions about policy, scoring, or expectations of the course:

Answer: Generally, the answer to this question is NO unless there is a documented emergency (i.e. death in the family, military deployment, admitted to the hospital, etc.) that can be documented. Without any documentation supporting the emergency doesn’t change the policy in the course.

Answer:  In 25+ years of teaching and facilitating classes I have never offered extra credit. Answer: See the answer to Question 1.

Answer: PC/Internet issues are the responsibility of the student to ensure you have access for the entire course. I would always recommend having a "Plan B" in terms of having alternative Internet access (i.e. McDonald's, Starbucks, Library, etc.) and/or an alternative PC/Smart Device you can get to during the remaining part of the course if you run into any issues.
