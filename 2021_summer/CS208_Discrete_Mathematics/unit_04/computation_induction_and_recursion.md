# Unit 4: Computation, Induction, and Recursion

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Computation, Induction and Recursion.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Analyze various algorithms. (CLO 1)

Compute the complexity of various algorithms. (CLO 1)

Examine various kinds of sequences. (CLO 1)

Formulate recurrence relations. (CLO 1)

Practice using Induction proving technique. (CLO 1)
