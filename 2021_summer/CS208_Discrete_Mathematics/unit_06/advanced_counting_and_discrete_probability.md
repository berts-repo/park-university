# Unit 6: Advanced Counting and Discrete Probability

Introduction

In this unit, you will be introduced to Advanced Counting and Discrete Probability.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Examine permutations and combinations. (CLO 1 and 4)

Identify binomial coefficients and combinatorial identities. (CLO 1 and 4)

Identify the probability of an event. (CLO 1 and 4)

Apply conditional probability and independence. (CLO 1 and 4)
