# Unit 5: Overview

[link: https://secure.flickr.com/photos/60852899@N07/14568955953] Introduction

In Unit 5, Quinn expands the conversation about information privacy into the realm of government surveillance and data mining.  He then explores computer and network security threats and crimes. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

List the provisions of the US Patriot Act. (CLO 2)

Identify methods of collecting data by the government. (CLO 2)

Examine the scope and scale of cybercrime. (CLO 2)

Differentiate between cybercrime and traditional crime. (CLO 3)

Describe the electronic trail left behind after or during everyday activities. (CLO 3)

Evaluate the significance of Cyber Espionage. (CLO 2)
