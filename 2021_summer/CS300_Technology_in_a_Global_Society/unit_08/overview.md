At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Describe the Internet of Everything. (CLO 2)

Analyze the critical role of data analysis as the Internet of Everything grows. (CLO 2)

Identify emerging roles in the future for the Internet of Everything. (CLO 3)

Explain how the “Internet of Everything” presents a crisis in cyber security. (CLO 3)

Summarize the “adoption curve” of new technologies. (CLO 1)
