Hey class,

I do not think people should be held responsible if they disseminate false information on blogs, although I do think there should more accountable, if someone post counter information. You often see post deleted, if there is a disagreement between two different viewpoints. I don't know how you would monitor something like this, and often it could be considered cyber bulling if nice words are not being used, which is often the case. I also don't think cyber bulling should be a crime unless a direct threat is made. We have always grown up with the saying "sticks and stones may break my bones but words will never hurt me." I believe this should be the case with cyber bullying as well. Sure, it may cause emotional damage to people, but untimely it depends on the person will, if it effects them or not. We should just raise awareness and allow users to block post from certain people.
