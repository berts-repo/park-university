# Unit 3: Overview

Introduction

Networked communications have expanded our world in unimaginable ways, but the same networked communications have put personal security at risk. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Debate the risks and reward of maintaining an online presence. (CLO 2)

Explain how information available on the Internet causes tension between free speech rights and privacy rights. (CLO 3)

Examine the world-wide implications of a disruption of internet communications. (CLO 2)

Predict the security risks of having personal information in cyberspace. (CLO 3)

Summarize forms of censorship. (CLO 2)

Analyze Internet addiction and the impact it has on society. (CLO 3)
