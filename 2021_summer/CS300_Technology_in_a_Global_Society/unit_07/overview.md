# Unit 7: Overview

Introduction

The focus of Unit 7 is globalization and national security. Jobs, robots, and the digital divide are all discussed In Quinn.  

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Discuss how information technology has impacted work and wealth. (CLO 2)

Debate the positive and negative effects of globalization. (CLO 2)

Discuss the “Winner-Take-All society. (CLO 3)
