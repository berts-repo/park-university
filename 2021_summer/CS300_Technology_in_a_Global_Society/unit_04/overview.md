Introduction

Intellectual property rights have been under siege in our information age. The ease of acquisition of online material has led to conflicting views of what is acceptable use. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Discuss intellectual property rights safeguards for intellectual property. (CLO 2)

Summarize the five principals of copyrights. (CLO 2)

Describe “Fair Use” as defined by the judicial system. (CLO 4)

Explain the Digital Millennium copyright Act. (CLO 4)

Examine the meaning of privacy. (CLO 2)

Describe the impact of data mining on society. (CLO 3)

Evaluate the marketing relevance of a generation that has grown up with social media. (CLO 3)

Write a research paper about one aspect of the history of technology as it relates to computer science. (CLO 1)
