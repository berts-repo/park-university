# Unit 2: Overview

[link: https://secure.flickr.com/photos/14908491@N02/8958807534] Introduction

The content of Unit 2 in Quinn provides an introduction to Ethics. In this age of social media and the uncharted waters of the age of technology, we need to revisit accepted moral guidelines and decide how to apply them to new problems. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Compare theories of ethical decision making. (CLO 4)

Describe the characteristics of both a utopian and dystopian futures. (CLO 3)

Recognize the tools and methods used by governments and businesses for data collection. (CLO 2)
