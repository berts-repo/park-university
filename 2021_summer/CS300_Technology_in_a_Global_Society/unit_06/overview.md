In Unit 6 the topics of computer reliability and professional ethics are both discussed by Quinn and the Software Engineering Code of Ethics is included in Chapter 9.  

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Identify vulnerabilities of networked computers. (CLO 2)

Examine the dangers of system and software failures. (CLO 2)

Describe the importance of computer simulations in science and engineering. (CLO 2)

Analyze the concepts and the purpose of the Software Engineering Code of Ethics and Professional Practice. (CLO 4)

Discuss the impact of “Whistle Blowing” on society. (CLO 4)
