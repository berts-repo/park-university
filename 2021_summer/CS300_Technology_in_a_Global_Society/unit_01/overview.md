# Unit 1: Overview

Introduction

The focus of study in Unit 1 is the historical background of Information Technology and an introduction to current topics. In addition, students will become familiar with the requirements and activities related to the course.

Unit Learning Outcomes

Click  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/61006/files/8044508/download?verifier=zF9CvgfRYIiy5GkPX66eXrJef8jsWpK2OkQFlciR&wrap=1] here to view Core Learning Objectives (CLOs)

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Discuss the role of ethics in the manipulation of personal data on the Internet. (CLO 4)

Define the Internet of Things. (CLO 2)

Review the historical development of technologies and their impact on society. (CLO 1)

Explain the impact of modern technology on our sense of community and our changing patterns of association. (CLO 3)

Review a current article(s) related to Technology. (CLO 3)

Apply Neil Postman’s framework for analyzing the introduction of new technologies. (CLO 4)
