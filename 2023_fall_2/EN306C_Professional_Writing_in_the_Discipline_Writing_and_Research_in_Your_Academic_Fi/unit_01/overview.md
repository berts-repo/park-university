# Unit 1: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn some basic ideas about academic writing and what academic disciplines are.  You will also learn how to read and break down complex academic articles and how to reconstruct them into your own effective summaries.  You will also preview your final assignment in the course so you can start thinking about ideas for your final project. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Read and summarize academic writing effectively (CLO 1 and 2)

Articulate and analyze general academic writing conventions (CLO 1)
