# Programs in Professional and Technical Writing

Have you considered earning a major, minor, or certificate in Professional and Technical Writing?

By completing this course, you're well on your way!

The English and Professional Writing major is a full degree program available to both on-campus and distance Park students.

The Professional and Technical Writing minor is a 6-course program perfect for anyone wanting to supplement their degree with in-demand career skills.

Already have a minor? The Professional and Technical Writing certificate is a 4-course program you can finish in no time!

Talk to your advisor or contact your instructor for more information.
