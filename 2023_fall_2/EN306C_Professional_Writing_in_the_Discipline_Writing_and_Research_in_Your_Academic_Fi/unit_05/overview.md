# Unit 5: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn about visual elements of academic writing, analyze the use of visuals in your discipline in this unit’s discussion, submit your interview questions for quiz credit, learn some organizational tips, and revise and submit the final draft of your researched analysis essay. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Effectively revise a draft using feedback from other readers (CLO 2)

Analyze an academic article using rhetorical principles (CLO 1)

Develop a specific analytical thesis statement (CLO 2)

Support thesis effectively with evidence and analysis (CLO 2)

Effectively use a secondary source to analyze a primary source (CLO 3)

Use correct citation format for in-text citations (CLO 3)

Analyze and understand visuals and data visualizations used in academic writing (CLO 1)
