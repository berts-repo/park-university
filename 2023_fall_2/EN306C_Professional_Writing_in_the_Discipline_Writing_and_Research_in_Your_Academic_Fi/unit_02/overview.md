# Unit 2: Overview

Introduction

In thus unit, you will read and begin working on the Unit 7 interview assignment, learn how to find and review journals in your discipline, begin to distinguish writing conventions in your discipline from other disciplines, and learn about plagiarism and how to avoid it. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Articulate and analyze discipline-specific writing conventions (CLO 1)

Locate and analyze disciplinary-specific, peer-reviewed journals in the student’s field of study (CLO 1 and 3)
