# Unit 6: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn about the value of peer response and how to respond effectively to your classmates’ writing in preparation for your peer response activity in the next unit.  You will also consider how disciplines differ in terms of organization strategies, formats, and genres, and you should have finished your interview by the end of the unit in preparation for submitting your interview write-up in the next unit.  Your first draft of your final researched analysis essay is due at the end of the unit. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Develop a specific analytical thesis statement (CLO 1 and 2)

Support thesis effectively with evidence and analysis (CLO 2)

Organize information effectively for the task and audience (CLO 2)

Use and cite sources effectively (CLO 3)

Analyze and understand disciplinary conventions of organization and format (CLO 1)
