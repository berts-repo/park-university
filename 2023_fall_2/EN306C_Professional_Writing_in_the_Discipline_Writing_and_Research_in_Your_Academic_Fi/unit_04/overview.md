# Unit 4: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn how work on your thesis statement in your analysis essay, learn how to find and evaluate sources, get feedback on your final project idea in discussion, submit your interview questions for your expert interview, and submit your proposal and bibliography at the end of the unit. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Develop a topic proposal for a researched analysis (CLO 2)

Identify and evaluate useful sources (CLO 3)

Use a citation format to correctly cite sources (CLO 3)

Develop effective interview questions for a disciplinary expert (CLO 1)
