In the second part I want to focus on the prompt: “What can you observe about the way visual rhetoric is used in the article including document design and layout as well as actual images or data visualizations?”

The authors build credibility and rhetorical ethos, by referencing others established work and previous research, the authors prove that their study is established in pre-existing knowledge and theories of the academic conversation. For instance, they cite BotMiner to explain a specific botnet detection approach, to support their discussion about the collective behavior of individual bots in a botnet (Obeidat & Yaqbeh, 2022). This adds to the authors credibility of their own research by showing that it is part of an ongoing scholarly conversation. The author’s entire second section, “2. Related Work”, compares their approach with previous research methodologies. For example, they discuss the use of data mining-based methods and genetic algorithms, highlighting the differences and improvements in their own approach. (Obeidat & Yaqbeh, 2022)

The authors of my article utilize various visual organizers to portray their concepts regarding genetic algorithms and neural networks, to analyze network packets and identify potential botnet activity. By breaking down their “3. Methodology” section into visual organizers, that include sections, ordered lists, models, figures, tables, formulas, and whitespace. The authors are able to articulate the various sections into a format that is easier for the reader to digest (Bahls et al., 2011).
