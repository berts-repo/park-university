# Unit 3: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will learn what rhetoric is, how to rhetorically analyze writing in your discipline, and read some professional scholars’ rhetorical analyses of academic writing to help you prepare your first draft of your rhetorical analysis essay.  You will also begin developing interview questions for your expert and your quiz this week will ask you to confirm that you have set up an interview with your chosen expert. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Analyze an academic article using rhetorical principles (CLO 1)

Develop a specific analytical thesis statement (CLO 2)

Support thesis effectively with evidence and analysis (CLO 2)

Effectively use a secondary source to analyze a primary source (CLO 3)
