Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Revise a researched analysis using feedback from other readers (CLO 2)

Demonstrate effective editing techniques for grammar and style (CLO 2)

Develop a specific analytical thesis statement (CLO 1 and 2)

Support thesis effectively with evidence and analysis (CLO 2)

Organize information effectively for the task and audience (CLO 2)

Use and cite sources effectively (CLO 3)

Student Opinion of Teaching Survey

During this unit you will have access to the online Student Opinion of Teaching Survey for your courses. When you open the Survey page, you will see “Load Student Opinion of Teaching Survey in a new window” – please click this button. In the new window that opens you will see a list of courses for which you have surveys to complete. Each course evaluation should take no longer than 15 minutes to complete. You are be able to access your surveys multiple times to edit answers or to complete saved surveys. Surveys must be completed by the last day of the course, 11:59 p.m., central.

Please be assured that your responses are presented anonymously to your instructors, and will be compiled for the university via a third party provider, Campus Labs. All student responses will be summarized and reported to your instructor(s) after the term is over and grades have been posted.

If you have any questions or technical problems, please contact  [link: mailto:assessment@park.edu] assessment@park.edu. Emails are answered 8:00 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. (central) Monday through Friday.
