# Unit 8: Overview

Introduction

AI is a powerful technology that can and will impact the direction of human civilization. Given the stakes involved, understanding the ethical implications of applying AI technologies is critical for future AI engineers. In this unit you will explore these ethical implications along with the philosophical underpinnings of the field. You will discuss the potential future impact of these technologies and the ethical concerns that carry forward with your peers, and in your homework assignment respond to a classical debate on the nature of intelligence. In this unit you will also be examined over the breadth of the course in your final exam and see how your game-playing agent fares in the class tournament!

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Contrast Turing and Searle’s perspectives on strong AI (CLO 2)

Analyze the ethical impact of substituting an AI technique for human judgement in a given scenario (CLO 2)

Predict future directions of AI and its associated challenges (CLO 2)
