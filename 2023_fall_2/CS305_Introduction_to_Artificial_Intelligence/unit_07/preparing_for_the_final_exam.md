# Preparing for the Final Exam

During Unit 8, you will take the final exam through Honorlock. This exam is worth 300 points and will be comprehensive. You will not be allowed to use notes or your book during the exam. You may use a small whiteboard or single sheet of blank paper in a transparent sleeve and a dry erase marker for scratch paper. A simple calculator will be provided through Honorlock.

110 points on the final exam will come from questions selected with the same criteria for the questions in each prior unit quiz. Thus, preparing for and taking the quizzes will help you prepare for the final exam. Review these quizzes as part of your preparation for the final.

There will also be 5 problems that will involve significant work developing a solution (developing a model for a knowledge base, evaluating an ML model, developing a naive bayes model, developing a minimax search tree, and developing a simple ontology), each worth 20 points each.

There will also be two essay questions spanning multiple units, each worth 45 points. These questions will seek a broad understanding of the course concepts and how you can apply them to problems.

There will not be any python programming on the exam.
