# Unit 6: Discussion - Learning Problem Classification

Introduction

In this assignment, you’ll consider the specifics of problems solvable by AI learning techniques and how those details specify what category of learning problem and thus ML models are most applicable. You’ll work with your peers to explore these concepts and form a better understanding of the breadth of machine learning.

Directions

Before beginning this assignment, complete the reading assignment.

Your group should first identify if each problem is best characterized as a “supervised”, “unsupervised”, or “semi-supervised” (reinforcement) problem and then briefly describe an appropriate algorithm (from your reading or another cited source). Use the discussion forum to collaborate towards answers for your prompt. 

After Thursday, take a look at some of the work performed by other groups. Comment on some of the work where you think you can help them improve their responses, or if you only agree with their work and comments from others, explain your support and elaborate beyond your classmates’ replies if possible. 

Your classmates and possibly also your instructor should provide you with feedback by Saturday afternoon. If there are important issues with your work that need to be corrected, work with your group to produce an improved response to the original prompt.
