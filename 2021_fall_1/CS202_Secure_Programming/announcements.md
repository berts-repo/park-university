## Week 3 -  Numbers! (It was my understanding there would be no math)
*Posted: 2021-08-30T19:44:28Z*

In this week we will review the use of integers and floats in C/C++. There are potential security issues that a program may have when improper checking of their use is present. Numbers operate within the constraints imposed by the hardware and OS of the computer system in which they are used.

The type of number used by a computer, and there are limits on the size that a number can be. Manipulating numbers without careful diligence to constraints provides a goldmine vulnerabilities in C/C++. You will be exposed to vulnerabilities and the way to remediate them.

You will learn about numeric issues such as:

Representation, Wrapping, Operations, and Conversion of Signed and Unsigned Integers

Representation, Operations and Conversion of Floating Point Numbers

Available C Mathematical Libraries and their functions
