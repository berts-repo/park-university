# Virtual Cafe

This is your student Virtual Café. A Virtual Café is comparable to a break room or a lounge that students talk in when the class is on a break.

Please use this Virtual Café to communicate with your fellow students about topics outside of the course unit discussions. 

You may also ask questions here regarding the general course(questions not related to the unit content).

Feel free to share links, recent news events, or other course related content. 

You may also find information that would be useful to other students. 

Please maintain professional decorum and netiquette. 

Again, you can refer to the Netiquette information on the Park Policies page.

Although I have access to the cafe, and may occasionally visit the cafe to monitor the discussions and make sure that appropriate netiquette is used, please don't assume that I will read every posting in this section immediately.

If you want me to read something or answer a question, please post your question on the Office page.
