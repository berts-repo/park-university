# Unit 2: Overview

Introduction

In this unit we will review two important C/C++ Control Statements:  Conditional and Repetition statements. We will review the topic of Boolean Expressions and incorporate them in conditional statements like the if-else structures. Boolean expressions also will be incorporated in repetition statements like the while, for, and do-while loops. The switch statement will also be studied.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

ULO#1 : Read, understand, and write C/C++ programs that use conditional and repetition statements to solve problems, without syntax or logical errors. (CLO1).
