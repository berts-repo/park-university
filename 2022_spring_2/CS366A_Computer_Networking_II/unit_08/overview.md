# Unit 8: Overview

Final Unit

Unit Learning Outcomes

In this course, you have learned about the structure and the operations of the modern networks. During this unit, you will take one comprehensive final exam, and one comprehensive Netlab skills exam.

CLO#1. Differentiate the functionalities of the network layers and protocols.

CLO#2. Contrast different network media, topologies, and devices.

CLO#3. Carry out network address calculations.

CLO#4. Construct networks with various networking elements.

CLO#5. Analyze network packet traces.
