# Unit 4: Overview

Cisco CCNA Introduction to Networks v7 (ITN) Module 14

Unit Learning Outcomes

In this unit, we will learn about the transport layer.

ULO#1: Examine the purpose of the transport layer (CLO 1 & 5)

ULO#2: Compare the operations of transport layer protocols in supporting end-to-end communication (CLO 1, 2, & 5)
