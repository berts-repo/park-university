Guidelines

Initial Post:

Answer one of the questions. Whenever possible, pick a question that has not been answered, or has an incorrect/incomplete answer. Provide a copy of the original question and question # in your response.

Follow-up posting

Each follow-up post should follow the same guidelines used for the initial post. Do not just say “you’re right/wrong” or “agree/disagree”. Support your feedback with a reasonable explanation. Your follow-up post should continue or expand the discussion, not just repeat the other student’s statement. You may have a different answer, or a similar answer with different/clearer reasoning.
