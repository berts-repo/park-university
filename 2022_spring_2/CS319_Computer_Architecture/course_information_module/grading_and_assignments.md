Late Submission Policy

All assignments are due 11:59 PM Central Time on dates specified in the course shell. No late submissions will be accepted except for reasonable circumstances approved by the instructor ahead of time (whenever possible).

Discussion

Discussion Questions - There will be one or two discussion threads in each unit. All discussion questions are worth 10 points, and responses need to address the core topic(s) of the question and answer follow up questions posted by the instructor.  Initial posts are due by 11:59 p.m. Wednesday, CT (Central Time).  After posting your answers, begin making 2 or more replies to other students' posts.
