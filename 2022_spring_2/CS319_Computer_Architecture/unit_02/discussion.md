3. Convert -18.5 to IEEE 754 32-bit format.

 

convert 18.5 to unsigned binary: 10010.1

sign bit for negative: 1

normalize unsigned binary: 1.00101 * (2^4)

drop the 1 for fraction bits: 00101

biased exponent: 4+127 = 131 = 128+2+1 = 0b10000011

combine sign bit, biased exponent, and fraction bits: 1 10000011 00101000000000000000000
