Introduction

Cisco Networking Academy provides a Final exam, which is required in this course. It has a time limit of 75 minutes. It can be taken only once. Each exam consists of approximately 60 multiple choice, multiple answer, and matching questions.

You will be asked to complete a “End of course Feedback” survey in order to take the final exam.
