# Unit 6: Deadlocks

Overview

One of the main problems an operating system encounters is the sharing of resources and deadlock becomes a real possibility. In this unit, you will study in detail the subject of deadlock, including how it occurs, how to detect and recover from it, how to avoid and prevent it.

Learning Objectives: Upon completing this unit, students will:

Examine the concepts of mechanisms of concurrency

Compare concurrency implementation in Linux and Windows

Examine the concepts principles of deadlock

Predict how to prevent and avoid deadlock

Write a program to obtain system information in Linux
