Late Assignment Policy

All assignments are due 11:59 PM Central Time on Sunday of the academic week. There will be 10% penalty for each day that the assignment is late.

Discussion

Discussion Questions (150 points total) - There will be two discussion questions in Units 1-7, and one discussion question during Unit 8.  All discussion questions are worth 10 points, and responses need to address the core topic(s) of the question and answer follow up questions posted by the instructor. 

Initial posts are due by 11:59 p.m. Thursday, CT (Central Time).  After posting your answers, begin making 2 or more replies to other students' posts.
