# Unit 1: Introduction to operating systems

Learning Objectives

Upon completing this unit, students will:

Construct the basic elements of Operating Systems

Summarize the history of Operating Systems

Examine the main objectives and functions of Operating Systems

Compare the structures for Operating Systems, operating in different system environments

Assess the structure of memory, registers, interrupts, and system calls

Differentiate the basic elements of the Linux and Windows Vista operating systems.
