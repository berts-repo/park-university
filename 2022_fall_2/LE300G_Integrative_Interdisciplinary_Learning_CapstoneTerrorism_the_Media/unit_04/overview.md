# Unit 4: Overview

Introduction

In this unit we look at the images or moving pictures journalists publish or broadcast in light of agenda setting and framing theories.

Students will turn in their Media Journal assignment by the end of this unit and complete reading Quiz 2 on chapters 3, 4 and 5 in the course textbook. 

The unit’s discussion gives students a chance to apply what they have learned about the difficulty of defining terrorism.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Define terrorism (CLO 1)

Synthesize how political science, communication, and other fields of study may be used to better understand the relationship between terrorism and media (CLO 5)

Explain the role media (ideally) play in U.S. democracy (CLO 2)

Explain the roles media play in terrorist acts (CLO 3)
