# Unit 3: Overview

Introduction

This unit delves further into the news media and terrorism relationship. We look at theories from the fields of political science and media studies. For your Discussion this unit you will apply what you are learning about media theories to a video of live uncut news footage from six different news stations which aired the morning of 9/11.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the roles media play in terrorist acts (CLO 3)

Synthesize how political science, communication, and other fields of study may be used to better understand the relationship between terrorism and the media (CLO 5)

Explain the role media (ideally) play in U.S. democracy (CLO2)

Define terrorism (CLO 1)
