Discussions

There will be one discussion per unit (25 points). Initial posts or responses should be 100-150 words and thoroughly answer all aspects of the prompt. Initial posts are due by 11:59 p.m. Thursday, CT (Central Time).  After posting your answers, begin making 2 or more replies to other students' posts. Strive to add to the conversation in a way that connects to the course material and directly responds to the prompt. Grading rubric is provided.

Media Analysis Journal

Due in Unit 4. (50 points) You will analyze depiction of media issues in a film based on given criteria. Assignment details will be posted in the Course Information Module under  [link: https://canvas.park.edu/courses/71314/pages/media-analysis-journal-guidelines] Media Analysis Journal Guidelines. A list of approved films is also on that page. Students are advised to pick a film and order it as soon as they begin the course. Journal will be due by 11:59 p.m. Sunday, CT (Central Time) Unit 4. The analysis should be 1 page, single spaced.
