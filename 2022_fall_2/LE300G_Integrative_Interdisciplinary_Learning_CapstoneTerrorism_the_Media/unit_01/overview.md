# Unit 1: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, to better understand the relationship between media and terrorism we begin by focusing on the media, what we mean when we talk about “the media.” We will consider professional codes of ethics and practice in journalism, the historic function and role of the press the U.S. We will look at the way journalists go about doing their jobs when they are not in crisis mode, when the nation is not under attack.

To jumpstart our efforts to learn more about how news is produced and the economic constraints on media companies in the current digital media era, we will view and discuss the Page One: Inside The New York Times, which will be our topic in Discussion this unit.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Identify current issues in journalism (CLO1, CLO2)
