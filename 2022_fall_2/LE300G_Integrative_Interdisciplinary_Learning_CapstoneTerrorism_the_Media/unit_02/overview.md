# Unit 2: Overview

Introduction

Last unit discussed the difficulties in defining what we mean when we talk about media. In this unit we look at why definitions of terrorism are equally problematic.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Define terrorism (CLO 2)

Compare distinguishing characteristics of different forms of media including social media related to coverage of terrorism ULO (CLO 3)

Synthesize how political science, communication, and other fields of study may be used to better understand the relationship between terrorism and the media. ULO (CLO 5)
