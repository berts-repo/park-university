# Unit 8: Overview

Introduction

Welcome to the last unit of Terrorism and the Media. The last lecture is devoted to social media and terrorism and the emergence of the citizen journalist, and the ongoing problematic definition of what is terrorism.

This unit you should devote most of your time to finishing your research paper, which is due at the end of this unit.

You will read the last chapter in the course textbook and complete the final exam.

This unit’s Discussion asks you to read and then comment on an article about social media’s impact on news coverage of the London Subway Bomb attacks in 2005.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Define terrorism (CLO 1, 2)

Compare distinguishing characteristics of different forms of media including social media related to the coverage of terrorism (CLO 3)

Explain the roles media play in terrorist acts (CLO 5)

Synthesize how political science, communication, and other fields of study may be used to better understand the relationship between terrorism and the media (CLO 4)
