# Unit 7: Overview

Introduction

This unit augments material in chapters 6 & 7 in the required textbook on investigative journalism and why critical terrorism coverage can be considered unpatriotic especially after an attack when nationalistic sentiments soar.

This unit’s discussion includes reading and then commenting on a piece of investigative journalism on the media appearances of alleged military analysts who may have played a role in garnering support for the war in Iraq.

Students will read chapters 6 and 7 in the course textbook and complete a reading quiz on the material.

Students should also be working this work on their research paper, due the end of Unit 8.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Identify current issues in journalism (CLO 1, CLO 2. CLO 4)

Explain the role media (ideally) play in U.S. democracy (CLO 2)
