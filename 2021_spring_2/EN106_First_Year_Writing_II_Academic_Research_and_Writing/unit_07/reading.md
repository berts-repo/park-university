# Unit 7: Reading

Required

Lunsford, "Sharing and Reflecting on Your Writing" and other readings as assigned - check with your instructor.

The chapter on creating portfolios from Easy Writer will help you consider how to construct your portfolio this unit. Pay close attention to the tips for assembling your portfolio.

Your instructor will also assign other readings from Easy Writer, to help you edit and proofread your work for lower-order concerns. Check the announcements for specific reading assignments.

Recommended

 [link: https://wts.indiana.edu/writing-guides/proofreading-grammar.html]  “Proofreading for Common Surface Errors,”  Indiana University.  

This website reviews common surface errors and how to correct them.

 [link: https://writing.colostate.edu/guides/guide.cfm?guideid=45] "Editing and Proofreading," Colorado State University.

This website, sponsored by the writing center at Colorado State University, offers tips for line-editing and proofreading your work.

 [link: https://grammarteaching.wordpress.com/2013/04/01/where-do-errors-come-from/] "Where do Errors Come From?" Grammar Pedagogy for Writing Teachers

This blog post, composed by an English professor, explains a few of the common theories about where, when, why, and how writers make errors. As it turns out, even the experts aren't sure why we make writing mistakes!
