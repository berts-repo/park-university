# Unit 1: Overview 

Habits of Mind of Academic Writers

This unit, we will work to define academic writing and to understand and implement the “habits of mind” (Greene and Lidinsky’s term) successful academic writers employ. Our readings and lecture material will introduce the concept of the rhetorical situation as a tool for practicing these habits of mind.

Unit Learning Outcomes

Explore our classroom space while getting acquainted with other students in the course.

Familiarize yourself with the course policies, workload and pace of study in EN106.

Apply the rhetorical situation as a framework for analyzing and interpreting a piece of writing.

Consider the rhetorical situations faced by academic writers to respond appropriately in both writing and research (CLO #4).

Apply writing processes, collaborative strategies, and effective academic research practices to participate in academic discourse (CLO #1).
