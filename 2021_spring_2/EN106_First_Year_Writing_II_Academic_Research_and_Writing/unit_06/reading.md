# Unit 6: Reading

Required

Greene & Lidinsky, “From Synthesis to Researched Argument,” (Ch. 8), and “From Revising to Editing: Working with Peer Groups,” (Ch. 12).

In Chapter Eight, Greene and Lidinsky teach several new methods for integrating outside sources into your writing. Equipped with this knowledge, you can ensure that your revision this unit makes an evidence-based argument that includes outside perspectives effectively.

Chapter 12 relates to your work in peer review. Pay special attention to the annotated drafts from students that appear in this chapter.

Other assigned readings from Lunsford or Greene & Lindinsky - check with your instructor

At this point in the term, your instructor will probably assign additional required readings, to help you succeed in your revision project this unit. Your instructor will notify you of additional readings via email or Announcements.
