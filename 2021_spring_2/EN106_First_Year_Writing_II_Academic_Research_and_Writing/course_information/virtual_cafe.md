# Virtual Cafe

where the great conversation makes up for the lack of coffee!

This discussion area provides a space for informal discussions and collaboration. Just as in most face-to-face classes, conversation here isn't limited to the class topics.

The discussion area is for you to discuss anything you wish, from essay ideas to favorite books and movies. Many of you might also wish to share writing projects you are working on outside of class--poems, short stories, song lyrics...

Participation in this area is completely optional and ungraded. This is your space--no instructor allowed (unless invited).
