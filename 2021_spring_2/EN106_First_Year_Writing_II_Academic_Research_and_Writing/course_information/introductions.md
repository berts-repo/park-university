# Introductions

Welcome!

We'll use this area to get to know one another.  Following the directions below, please post an introduction sometime by the middle of the first unit of class.

In your post, please make sure to cover the following three areas:

Tell us a little about your academic self--your major, year in school, and, most importantly, what you would like to gain from EN106, what will make this course worthwhile to you.
 

Tell us something about you as a person outside of academia--hobbies, interests, family, pets.
 

End your post by devising either a metaphor or simile that describes who you are as a writer (e.g., "As a writer, I am a turtle. I write very slowly, but I always win the race" or "As a writer, I am like an archeologist. I search and search and search to find the perfect jewel of an idea.")

Please post your introductory information below and then return later, or tomorrow, to read and respond to other students' introductions.
