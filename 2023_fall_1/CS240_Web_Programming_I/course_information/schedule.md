Due Dates

All due dates and times in the course are based on CST, Central Standard Time Zone, because that is where the Parkville main campus is located.

Read and complete each Lesson and all related activities for each unit by Sunday by 11:59 pm

First post in the discussion is due on Thursday by 11:59 pm with reply postings to 2 other students by Sunday at 11:59 pm.

Homework assignments are due on Sunday by 11:59 pm.  No late homework is accepted. 

Final Exam is due on Sunday by 11:59 pm during Unit 8!

Reminders About Readings

Always read the lesson first. We will heavily use the book in this course. There are two main units to the course. The first focuses on HTML and CSS and the second focuses on web programming and processing forms with JavaScript. 

Dean, John. (2017) Web Programming with HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. Jones & Bartlett Learning 2017. ISBN: 9781284091793
