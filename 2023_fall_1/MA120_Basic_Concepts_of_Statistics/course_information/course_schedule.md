# Course Schedule

The course schedule below contains the unit reading assignments.  The types of activities that you will also be required to complete each week include written answers to discussion thread questions, with a response to a classmate's answer posting, an online homework assignment, and an online quiz.  In addition a study group area for students to ask questions related to that week's topic is provided.  The specific directions for each activity are located on the assignment page.

This course is split into 8 units. The unit starts and ends on Sunday at midnight CST (Central Standard Time zone, the time zone of the main Park University campus.) 

You will be able to view your course content on the first day of the week (Mondays).

The sequence of topics in the course is linear.  Complete Unit 1 before starting Unit 2 content.

Submitting Assignments
All electronic assignments are required to be submitted through the  Discussion threads, or the MyMathLab. 

Schedule Changes
Occasionally there are weather problems on campuses, technical outages or other problems that may interfere with a student having access to the online courses.  Instructors may modify these dates if needed.  Students will be notified of changes in the schedule in the weekly Course Announcements. 

Schedule
To stay on track, try to accomplish each task by the day indicated.  
No late assignments will be accepted after Sunday each week.  (See emergency only exceptions in Course Overview)

Due Dates Each Unit:

Discussion Thread – Unless directed by your instructor, Answer Posting is due by 11:59 PM Saturday; Response Posting is due by 11:59 PM Sunday. 

Homework and Quiz – Unless directed by your instructor, complete both before 11:59 PM CST on Sunday.
