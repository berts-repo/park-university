# Unit 8: Overview

Unit 8 HOME PAGE

Activities and Assessments:

This week is a review week. No new material to read and no homework and quiz. Students must take the comprehensive final exam in MyLab and Mastering.

a. Readings:
Review Chapters 1-8 in the textbook.

b. Concept Questions (10 pts)
Answer a question from the list of questions. The directions and the grading rubric are located in the Discussion area and the Course Overview. The discussion questions should provide a good preparation for the final exam.

c. Concept Questions (5 pts)
Respond to a classmate's answer posting. The directions and the grading rubric are located in the Discussion area and the Course Overview.

d. Final Exam (175 pts) - Access the final exam via the Honorlock link on the Canvas menu
The Course Assessment for this course is a final exam.  You will be allowed 2 hours to complete it. You may use a hand-held, non-graphing, non-programmable calculator. The final exam is located in Honorlock within the Canvas course.
