# Unit 2: Overview

Introduction

This unit explores the legal and regulatory environment and its relationship to information security. It examines the major national and international laws that affect the practice of information security, as well as the role of culture in ethics as it applies to information security professionals. Additionally, in this unit we engage in an introductory discussion of digital forensics.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

ULO 1: Differentiate between law and ethics (CLO3)
ULO 2: Describe the ethical foundations and approaches that underlie modern codes of ethics (CLO3)
ULO 3: Discuss relevant professional organizations and their role and relationship to organizational InfoSec (CLO4)
ULO 4: Identify significant national and international laws that relate to the practice of InfoSec (CLO3)
ULO 5: Explain the challenges and methods associated with the management of digital forensics in an organization (CLO3 and CLO5)
