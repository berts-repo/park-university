Course Details

Title:  Cybersecurity Management

Course Description: CJ316 Cybersecurity Management: This course will focus on the principles of security management with regard to how human interactions and interventions influence security protocols through actions. An examination of best practices in security management are explored through controlled exercises, and practical field application. The content will change to remain current. 

Credit Hours: 3:0:3

Prerequisite Courses: None

Introduction

This course applies the principles and practices explained and describe in Management of Information Security. As such part of the course focus is on the managerial aspects of information security, although there is continue coverage of governance and compliance issues. While retaining enough foundational material to allow reinforcement of key concepts, this course does not dwell excessively on technical examples. However, this course contains an in-depth discussion of the key managerial areas: risk management, information security governance, access control models, and information security program assessment and metrics.

Another part of the focus of this course is on the principles of security management with regard to how human interactions and interventions influence security protocols through actions. An examination of best practices in security management are explored through controlled exercises, and practical field application. This course has imbedded virtual lab assignments designed to further enhance the teaching and learning goals of this course, both as a stand-alone offering as well as a component to the cybersecurity certificate.

Core Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the course, learners will be able to:

Determine the role of the Cybersecurity Management in the enterprise

Discuss the Management of Information Security

Explore best practices for implementation of: Compliance, Laws and Ethics

Examine Governance and Strategic Planning for Cybersecurity and Information Security Policy

Create a Practical Cybersecurity Management Plan
o Risk Management: Assessing and Treating Risk
o Security Management Models and Practices
o Planning for Contingencies
o Security Maintenance
o Protection Mechanisms

Student Opinion of Teaching

Starting in Unit 7, you will have access to the online Student Opinion of Teaching Survey for your courses. When you open the Survey page, you will see “Load Student Opinion of Teaching Survey in a new window” – please click this button. You will be able to access your surveys multiple times to edit answers or to complete saved surveys. Surveys must be completed by the last day of the course, 11:59 p.m. CT.

Please be assured that your responses are presented anonymously to your instructors, and will be compiled for the university via a third party provider, Campus Labs. All student responses will be summarized and reported to your instructor(s) after the term is over and grades have been posted.
