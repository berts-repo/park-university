Course Details

Title: CS372 Advanced Networking 

Course Description:

This course introduces a variety of advanced networking concepts. Course topics include (but not limited to): enterprise networking, WAN, network security, and network management. Additionally, the course contains various hands-on networking projects. This course includes the concepts in a course recommended by Cisco for Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA).

In this course, you will access to Cisco Network Academy ( [link: http://www.netacad.com/] www.netacad.com ) and Netlab ( [link: http://labs.fhsu.edu] labs.fhsu.edu) for most of the course-related material.

Credit hours:  3:0:3

Prerequisites: CS371

Core Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the course, learners will be able to:

Examine OSPF routing protocol and WAN designs.

Apply security to networks.

Optimize, monitor, and troubleshoot networks.

Differentiate emerging network technologies.

Late Submission Policy

Assignment due dates will be provided when it is assigned. Below shows the policy for the assignments that are turned in late.

For all assignments in unit 8, late submission is not acceptable.

Park quizzes are due when specified. Late work is not accepted because quizzes will have had the answers revealed to the rest of the class.

Discussions may be made past the initial deadline with a 20% penalty per day. No discussion posting will be allowed after the Sunday deadline since no one else will be discussing the topic.

The rest of the assignments may be turned in late with a 20% penalty per day. Note that items turned in more than 4 days late will receive a 0.

Student Opinion of Teaching

Starting in Unit 7, you will have access to the online Student Opinion of Teaching Survey for your courses. When you open the Survey page, you will see “Load Student Opinion of Teaching Survey in a new window” – please click this button. In the new window that opens you will see a list of courses for which you have surveys to complete. Each course evaluation should take no longer than 15 minutes to complete. You will be able to access your surveys multiple times to edit answers or to complete saved surveys before the deadline.

Please be assured that your responses are presented anonymously to your instructors, and will be compiled for the university via a third party provider, Campus Labs. All student responses will be summarized and reported to your instructor(s) after the term is over and grades have been posted.
