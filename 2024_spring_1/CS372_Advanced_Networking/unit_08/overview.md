Unit Learning Outcomes

In this course, you have learned about enterprise networking, security, and automation. During this unit, you will take two final exams, and perform a Netlab skills assessment.

CLO#1. Examine OSPF routing protocol and WAN designs.

CLO#2. Apply security to networks.

CLO#3. Optimize, monitor, and troubleshoot networks.

CLO#4. Differentiate emerging network technologies.
