# Unit 1: Overview

Cisco CCNA – Enterprise Networking, Security, and Automation v7 Module 1 and Module 2

Unit Learning Outcomes

In this unit, we will learn about the single-area OSPFv2 concepts and configuration.

ULO#1: Explore how single-area OSPF operates in both point-to-point and broadcast multi-access networks (CLO 1 & 2)

ULO#2: Implement single-area OSPFv2 in both point-to-point and broadcast multi-access networks. (CLO 1 & 2)
