# Unit 2: Overview

Unit 2: Data Acquisition and Crime Scenes Processing

Introduction 

Data acquisition is the process of copying data. For digital forensics, it’s the task of collecting digital evidence from electronic media. There are two types of data acquisition: static acquisitions and live acquisitions. In this unit, you will learn how to perform static acquisitions from magnetic disk media and flash drives. Additionally, you will also learn how to process a digital investigation scene.
