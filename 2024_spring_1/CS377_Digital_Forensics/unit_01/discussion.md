Case 6: A hacker deploys a botnet to steal credit card information online.

Assessment

Has the threat actor’s computer been seized, that was used for command and control of the botnet?

Were there any other devices present at the crime scene (e.g. flash drives or laptops)?

Is there a crime scene?

Has he been arrested?

Do we have any of the victims’ computers that a “bot” was deployed on?

Do we have a list of compromised credit cards?

What locations were the attacks made (e.g. different states, and countries)?

Is there evidence of other crimes?

Note: I can keep adding questions to this list without more information on the case.

Design Approach and Checklist

Are there any risk assessment guidelines/policies/procedures in place?

Refer to an agency guideline and checklist for botnet mitigation.

Needed Resources

The signatures for the propagation of the botnet attack

Permission for scope of work (e.g. credit card companies records)

Network traffic logs (e.g. the traffic log where the command and control took place, as well as victims traffic logs)

Image of the threat actor’s device used for command and control.

Mitigating the risk

How was the botnet propagated? Can command and control be shut off, or can another threat continue.

Determine the backdoor and send out security alerts/updates.]

Does the public need to be informed if it can’t be stopped?

Test the Design

Test any images and hashes.

Compare IP addresses of the victims and the infected computers to the network log of the crime scene.

Compare signatures of the threat’s propagation triggers.

Analyzing the threat

Monitor and compare network traffic.

Checking for malware for botnet propagation.

Can the botnet be deployed by another means?

Was there signs or confidential information on this threat’s computer (e.g. card numbers)

Investigation findings

Compare credit card information matched records.

Does any network log data match up to the victims’ computers?

How the botnet deployed

Where command and control were operated from

Document and Critique

Document everything and also compare the investigation to other crimes related to botnet attacks
