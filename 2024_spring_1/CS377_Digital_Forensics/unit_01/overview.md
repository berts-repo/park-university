# Unit 1: Overview

Unit 1: Introduction and Laboratory Set Up

Introduction 

In the past several years, the field of computer forensics has developed significantly, including new terminology. In this unit, we will introduce you to digital forensics and give you an overview of how to manage a digital investigation and use standard problem-solving techniques. In addition, we will introduce some options for setting up an effective digital forensics laboratory, as well as the steps to consider budget and time when updating their labs to keep pace with computer technology changes.

 Click for Unit Learning Outcomes

ULO1: Explain how to prepare a digital forensics investigation by taking a systematic approach.

ULO2: Describe standard procedures for conducting forensic analysis.

ULO3: Apply different computer forensic tools to a given cybercrime scene.

UL43: Apply current practices to data recovery and acquisition.
