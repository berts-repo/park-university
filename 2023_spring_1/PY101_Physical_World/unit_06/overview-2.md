# Unit 6: Overview-2

Introduction

Unit 6 develops the ideas of Heat and thermal energy. We will discuss how heat is transferred and the concept of specific heat. We examine the first and second laws of thermodynamics and their significance to our world.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Define heat and temperature and explain the difference. (1)

Examine Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin scales. (2)

Calculate temperature change using specific heat. (1,6)

Discuss the different modes of heat transfer and theirs significance to everyday phenomena (greenhouse effect, solar power, etc.) (1,2,6)

Explain and Evaluate problems involving change of phase. (1,3 ,6)

Discuss the first and second laws of thermodynamics. (1,2,6)

Calculate the work of a heat engine based on a graph of pressure and volume. (1,3,6)
