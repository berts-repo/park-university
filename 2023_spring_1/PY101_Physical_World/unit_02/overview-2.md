# Unit 2: Overview-2

Introduction

Unit 2 continues our discussion of Newton’s Laws and puts them in to practice. These should begin to help you more precisely analyze the world around you. We will look at how to diagram a system so that we can focus on the forces that cause motion in the system. Later in the unit we will introduce the concept of momentum and how momentum is useful in understand and predicting behavior in colliding systems.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain what a force is. (1)

Diagram a physical system using force. (4,2)

Examine and use Newton’s second law. (1,5,6)

Examine and use Newton’s third law. (1, 5,6)

Explain and Evaluate problems involving Forces with 3rd Law (1 ,6)
