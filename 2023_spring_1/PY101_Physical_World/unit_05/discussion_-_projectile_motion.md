Without air resistance (drag), what is the launch angle that produces the largest range?

The angle that produces the largest range is 45 degree, this is because the angle half of a right angle, which means it splits the distance between the height and distance component.

If a launch angle of 32 degrees can reach the hole but a tree is in the way, what angle can be used to “lob” a golf ball over the tree to the hole?

To determine the angle to "lob" the golf ball over the tree would depend on a few variables: the height of the tree, the distance between the tree and hole, the distance to the hole, and the initial velocity of the golf ball. Because, the golf ball has to get over the tree it will have a longer hang time, which is more time for air resistance to affect the velocity of the ball.
