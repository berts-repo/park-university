# Unit 5: Overview-2

Introduction

Unit 5 will examine projectile and satellite systems. Both depend on the fundamental force of gravity.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain and Evaluate potential and kinetic energy for gravitational systems.(1,6)

Explain and Evaluate the similarity of behavior between projectile and satellite systems (1,6)
