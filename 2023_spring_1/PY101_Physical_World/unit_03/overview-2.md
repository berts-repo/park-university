# Unit 3: Overview-2

Introduction

Unit 3 gives us a new way of looking at the physical world around us. The concept of Energy allows us to look at a system (even those involving many particles) in a holistic manner. Often this way of analyzing a system can be simpler than the force view point. We will look at Kinetic and Potential energies and how doing Physical Work can change those quantities.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Define momentum. (1)

Explain and Evaluate problems involving conservation of momentum (collisions). (1 ,6)

Examine and explain the energy of a system using a graph of the potential energy. (2,3)

Explain and Evaluate problems involving kinetic energy. (1 ,6)

Explain and Evaluate problems involving physical work. (1 ,6)
