# Unit 7: Overview-2

Introduction

Unit 7 will introduce methods for measuring and describing waves of various types. We will look at the basic behavior of waves and the concept of resonance. We will then focus on sound waves and the ideas of frequency, intensity, pitch, and loudness.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the difference between Transverse and Longitudinal Waves. (1,2)

Calculate the change in frequency due to Doppler Effect. (1,6)

Explain the significance of resonance for the Tacoma Narrows bridge collapse. (2,5)

Examine and explain sound intensity and loudness.
