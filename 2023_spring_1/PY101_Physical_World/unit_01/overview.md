# Unit 1: Overview

Introduction

Unit 1 begins with a review of basic science ideals and measurements systems. We will then look at motion in 1 and 2 dimensions and describing the motion with the concepts of acceleration and velocity. 

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Evaluate a real world system using metric and imperial units and describe the forces involved.(1,4)

Explain the scientific method. (1,2,5)

Define position, velocity, and acceleration. (1)

Use Excel to graph position and velocity of particles moving in 1-d and 2-d. (3)

Use kinematic equations of motion with constant acceleration. (1,2,6)
