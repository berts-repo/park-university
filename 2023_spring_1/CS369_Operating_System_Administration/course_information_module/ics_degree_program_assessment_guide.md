External Assessment Exam

As part of program assessment, all students in CS 369 must take the ETS Major Field Test in Computer Science online before the last week of the term. This will be worth 4.5% of your grade in the course. More information about the test is available at:

 [link: https://www.ets.org/mft/about/content/computer_science] Sample questions

 [link: https://www.ets.org/mft/off_campus_testing/test_takers/policies] Testing information

If you haven’t received a welcome email from ETS by the start of the 3rd week of the course, contact your instructor or the program coordinator, ics.pc@park.edu.

To take the test you will need a Windows or Macintosh computer, a web-cam and microphone, and a wired Ethernet connection. You need to test your equipment at two different sites,  [link: http://etsreadiness.ets.org/] ETS: Check your Site Readiness and  [link: http://www.proctoru.com/testitout/] ProctorU Test It Out. Also be aware that ETS requires use of a secure browser, but this browser does NOT work inside a virtual machine, even if your VM passes all the other tests.

You must take the test by the end of unit 7. Tests must be scheduled at least 72 hours in advance. If you need to cancel or reschedule, this must be done at least 24 hours in advance. Failure to take a scheduled test results in a forfeiture of your test. If this happens, then you will be responsible for paying to schedule and take the test again. Although the test is 2 hours, please allow 3 hours in your schedule to allow for resolving technical issues as you begin your remote testing session.

If you have any question about the test, please consult with your instructor or the department program coordinator, ics.pc@park.edu.

Internal Graduation Survey

In addition to the MFT, students will also take a short, anonymous survey during the last week of the term. This survey asks about experiences and satisfaction with their course of studies. This survey is worth 0.5% of the course grade.
