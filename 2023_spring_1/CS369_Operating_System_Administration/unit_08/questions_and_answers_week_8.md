# Questions and Answers Week 8

The Q & A area is a non-graded area and is for students to communicate. This is your place to ask specific or technical questions about the concepts and topics in this unit. Think about this area as your class study group. The more everyone participates, the more help everyone gets. The Information Technology community is very much a part of sharing and helping bring everyone to new levels of knowledge and skills. Use this to help share, as well as to get to know each other better in each unit.
