# IS 361 - Live! Sessions

Dear Students,

In this course, we are providing Live! Synchronous sessions in an experimental fashion. You are invited to attend these Zoom sessions for guidance on the most important topics in the course. These sessions may last about 2 hours. They are optional. You are welcomed to attend, but not required to do so as part of your grade. If there is at least one student present, the Live! Session will be recorded for later viewing. To attend, go to the Zoom tab in the left Menu of your Canvas course and select the appropriate session link on the scheduled date and time. The password will be IS361Livex, where you must replace the letter x with the session number. For example, the password for the second session will be IS361Live2. Please do not share the password with anybody outside the class.
