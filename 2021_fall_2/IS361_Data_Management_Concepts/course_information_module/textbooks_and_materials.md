Required Software

Software title: Microsoft Access 2007 or higher

Instructions for acquiring the software:

Microsoft Access can be obtained in various ways:

It may be already in your computer. If you have Microsoft Office, it may be part of the suite. Check if you have a version in your computer.

You may buy the software. This is the most onerous and less recommended choice, since you have the following option.

Download the software from Microsoft Image Premium Webstore for free. As a Park Student, you should have received an invitation at the beginning of the semester regarding this service. If you have not yet activated your MS Image Premium account, you can log in to the following link with your Park Email account and password:  [link: https://e5.onthehub.com/WebStore/Welcome.aspx?ws=d6281bf6-9f15-e311-93f4-b8ca3a5db7a1] On the Hub: Park University. Follow the instructions to activate your account. If you already have an account, click the Start Shopping button after login to browse the catalog and download Microsoft Access. If you experience any problem with this system contact  [link: [REDACTED]@park.edu] [REDACTED]@park.edu for assistance.

Finally, you could get access to a Park Computer Lab, where the software will be installed. Park Computer Labs are located at various Park Campus Centers. However, you may also access a Virtual Park Computer Lab. Beginning in the 2017-18 school year, the lab applications are being hosted in a Citrix environment rather than being installed on each computer in all of the labs.

Before you can access this environment, your device will need to have the Citrix Receiver installed.  Please visit  [link: https://www.citrix.com/downloads/citrix-receiver/] Citrix-Receiver Downloads from your Windows, Mac, or Linux computer to download the appropriate version for your computer.  Citrix Receiver is also available from the Google Play store and the iTunes App Store for your mobile devices.   [link: https://www.citrix.com/products/receiver/] You can also visit this link to find help with installing Citrix Receiver on your PC, or your mobile device.

Once installed, visit our site at  [link: https://citrix.park.edu/] https://citrix.park.edu (from a web browser on your computer, or directly from the app on a mobile device).  

Enter your ID number as your username (please note:  if you are FT Faculty, or Staff, you will enter your username instead) and enter your password, then click the option to accept the terms and conditions, and click Log On.

Some of the more simple, common applications are available on the main screen.  Click on the Desktops option at the top of the screen to launch a published desktop that has all of the available applications on it.
