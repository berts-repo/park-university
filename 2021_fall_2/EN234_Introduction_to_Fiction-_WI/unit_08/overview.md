# Unit 8: Overview

Introduction

Genre and Experimental Fiction

In this unit, you will do the following:

Read the Overview and Lecture

Read the assigned texts

Write and submit your reading journal

Participate in the discussion (1 initial post, 2 responses)

Write and submit your final Core Assessment paper and Video Reflection

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the completion of this unit, the learner will be able to:

Compare literary genres. (CLO 1 and 2)

Articulate a claim about two fictional texts. (CLO 2 and 3)

Support the claim with evidence from the primary texts and outside sources. (CLO 1, 2, and 3)

Present a summary of their final paper. (CLO 3)

Reflect on what they have learned about fiction. ( CLO 1, 2, and 3)

Student Opinion of Teaching Survey

During this unit you will have access to the online Student Opinion of Teaching Survey for your courses. When you open the Survey page, you will see “Load Student Opinion of Teaching Survey in a new window” – please click this button. Please be assured that your responses are presented anonymously to your instructors, and will be compiled for the university via a third party provider, Campus Labs. All student responses will be summarized and reported to your instructor(s) after the term is over and grades have been posted.
