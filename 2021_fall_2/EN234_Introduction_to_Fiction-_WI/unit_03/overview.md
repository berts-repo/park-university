# Unit 3: Overview

Introduction

Genre and the Gothic’s Influence

In this unit, you will do the following:

Read the Overview and Lecture

Read the assigned texts

Write and submit your reading journal

Participate in the discussion (1 initial post, 2 responses)

Write and submit your Topic Proposal for the Midterm Project

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the completion of this unit, the learner will be able to:

Explore how experimental fiction maintains or diverges from traditional characteristics of fiction. (CLO 1 and 2)

Recognize features of fiction, which might include style and symbol. (CLO 1)

Sketch how a fictional text might be translated into a different artistic medium. (CLO 2 and 3)
