# Unit 4: Overview

Introduction

Genre, the Novella, and Magical Realism

In this unit, you will do the following:

Read the Overview and Lecture

Read the assigned texts

Write and submit your reading journal

Participate in the discussion (1 initial post, 2 responses)

Submit your Midterm Project, Reflection, and Video Presentation

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the completion of this unit, the learner will be able to:

Define genre. (CLO 1)

Compare literary genres. (CLO 1 and 2)

Design a project that creatively engages with fiction we have read in class. (CLO 3)

Reflect on how their creative project engages with the literature in new ways. (CLO 1, 2, and 3)
