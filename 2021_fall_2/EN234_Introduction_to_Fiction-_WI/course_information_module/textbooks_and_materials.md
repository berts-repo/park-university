Required Software

You will need word processing software (Microsoft Word) to complete some of your assignments. Please save your documents as .doc or .rtf files so they may be viewed by your instructor. Be sure to include your name and assignment title in the file name of all your course submissions. 

Park students have access to the Microsoft Office suite through Office 365, which is a free subscription plan that includes access to Microsoft Office applications as well as other productivity tools that are enabled over the Internet (cloud services). Users can install 365 on up to five devices (including Mac), tablets, and mobile devices. Go to  [link: https://login.microsoftonline.com/] Microsoft Online and use a Park user ID (student ID number, not user name) and password to log in.
