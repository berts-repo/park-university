Course Details

Title: EN234 Introduction to Fiction

Course Description: A close reading of selected works of English and American prose fiction, emphasizing the historical development of the novel and short story.

Credit Hours: 3:0:3

Prerequisite Courses: None

Core Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the course, learners will be able to:

Identify the definitive features of fiction as a literary genre.

Discuss and analyze a representative range of fictional texts.

Compose a response/ interpretation of a single text or body of related texts using appropriate literary tools.

Writing Intensive

This class is designated "Writing Intensive" and can be counted towards your required WI course outside your discipline. Writing Intensive means that there are significant writing assignments within the class as well as instruction on how to write effectively. For this class, we are focusing on writing about literature, but the skills you will learn will help you to consider all kinds of texts (not just fictional ones).
