# Unit 2: Overview

Introduction

Early Fiction

In this unit, you will do the following:

Read the Overview and Lecture

Read the assigned texts

Write and submit your reading journal

Participate in the discussion (1 initial post, 2 responses)

Write and submit Short Paper 1

Continue thinking about the Midterm Project

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the completion of this unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the characteristics and challenges of early fiction. (CLO 1 and 2)

Recognize features of fiction, which might include point of view and tone. (CLO 1)

Interpret how an early fictional text uses point of view or tone. (CLO 1, 2, and 3)
