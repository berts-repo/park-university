MariaDB DBMS - Instructions for acquiring the software:

Students may get access to MariaDB DBMS in two ways:

By accessing the Citrix Park Virtual Lab through the Internet, where the software is already installed.
Before you can access this environment, your device will need to have the Citrix Receiver installed.  Please visit  [link: https://www.citrix.com/downloads/citrix-receiver/] Citrix-Receiver Downloads from your Windows, Mac, or Linux computer to download the appropriate version for your computer.  Citrix Receiver is also available from the Google Play Store and the iTunes App Store for your mobile devices.   [link: https://www.citrix.com/products/receiver/] You can also visit this link to find help with installing Citrix Receiver on your PC or your mobile device.

Once installed, visit our site at  [link: https://citrix.park.edu/] https://citrix.park.edu (from a web browser on your computer, or directly from the app on a mobile device).  

Enter your ID number as your username (please note:  if you are FT Faculty or Staff, you will enter your username instead) and enter your password, then click the option to accept the terms and conditions, and click Log On.

Some of the more simple, common applications are available on the main screen. Click on the Desktops option at the top of the screen to launch a published desktop that has all of the available applications on it.

Click on the CS Lan icon:
 

This will take you to a virtual published desktop with all of the applications we have available for lab/classroom use.  Just like any Windows environment, double-click the application you wish to run to start it:

Contact Park Help Desk for any problem or question you may have about this environment.

There are two ways to get to MariaDB:

Using The MariaDB Client icon.

This is the easier and faster way to get to MariaDB. A control panel will appear and prompt you for a password to the root account. The password is simply “password"

Using the MariaDB Command Prompt.

This is basically the same, but here you need to start the MariaDB client on that control panel by issuing the following command:
mysql –u root – p
This will prompt you for a password (again password).

After providing the password you will be located in the root account of your database management system. Be mindful to store all your work on a local drive (a disk on your computer or a zip file on your computer). Tables created in the Citrix Park Virtual Lab may be destroyed once your session ends, so you will not find your files if you did not keep a copy in your local environment.

Contact Park Help Desk for any problem or question you may have about this environment.
