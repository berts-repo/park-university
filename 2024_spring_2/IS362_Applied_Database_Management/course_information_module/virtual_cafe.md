# Virtual Cafe

This is your student Virtual Café. A Virtual Café is comparable to a break room or a lounge that students talk in when the class is on a break.

Please use this Virtual Café to communicate with your fellow students about topics outside of the course unit discussions.

Feel free to share links, recent news events, or other course-related content.

You may also find information that would be useful to other students.

Please maintain professional decorum and netiquette.

Again, you can refer to the Netiquette information on the Park Policies link in the menu on the left-hand side of this page.

Although I have access to the cafe, and may occasionally visit the cafe, please don't assume that I will read every posting in this section. If you want me to read something or answer a question, please post it on the Instructors Office page.

Remember, this page is for you to communicate with your classmates about topics outside of the unit discussions.
