Once you completed your new ER Diagram, you need to convert it into a graphical representation of a database, AKA Relational Data Model. As we will see in this unit, this graphical representation uses a rectangle for each attribute, a set of rectangles to represent an entity (known as tables from this point on), and solid arrows between tables (entities) to support their relationship with foreign keys. Be sure that this Database Design is in the Third Normal Form. Include this new diagram of your design also in your initial reply.
