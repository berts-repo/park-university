Questions

This unit discussion’s questions are:

Give an example from your daily life of an entity that contains single attributes, composite attributes, and multivalue attributes. Give an example from your daily life of an entity that maintains a unary relationship with itself. Describe the entity and the relationship in English statements and show an ER diagram that describes the entity, its attributes, and the unary relationship with the correct ER notation.

Give an example from your daily life of pair of entities that maintains a binary relationship between them. Give an example from your daily life in which an entity has an attribute that could also be represented as a separate entity in a relationship with the first entity. Describe both ways to represent this attribute in English statements and show the ER diagrams that describe the two alternative representations.
