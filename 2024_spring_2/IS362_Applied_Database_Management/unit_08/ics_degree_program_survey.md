# ICS Degree Program Survey

**Due:** 2024-05-06T04:59:00Z
**Points:** 5.0

The information and Computer Science (ICS) degree program uses an internal graduation survey for assessing the program competencies. The internal graduation survey is designed by the ICS faculty. It constitutes 0.5% of the student's total grade in the course. Students are strongly encouraged to complete the survey, as it will help the ICS program to assess students’ expectations and satisfaction of the ICS curriculum. 

All survey responses are anonymous.   The only information that is associated with your account is whether you have completed the survey or not.
