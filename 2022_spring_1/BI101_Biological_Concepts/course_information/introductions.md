# Introductions

Introduce Yourself
Before you start the course, take a few moments to become acquainted with the Threaded Discussion tool by posting a short introduction.

Please tell us ....... 

(1) a little about yourself--where/what you do professionally, 
(2) something about you as a person outside of this course--hobbies, interests, family, pets, and
(3) something you have experienced as a result of your birth position (oldest, middle child, only child, etc).

Please post your introductory information below and then return later, or tomorrow, to read and respond to other participants' introductions.
