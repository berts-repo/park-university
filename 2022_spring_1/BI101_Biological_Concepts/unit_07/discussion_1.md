In natural selection individuals with certain traits are more likely to survive and reproduce than individuals with other traits. (Simon, 247) 

The conditions that must be met in order for natural selection to occur.

According to Darwin the key to understanding natural selection was through artificial selection, and the key two essential components are variation and heritability. (Simon 252)

How natural selection explains the scenario above.

In the case of the prompt, the fungicide with with fungal resistant genotype reproduces with fungicides with like traits, producing the fungicide that has become "worthless".
