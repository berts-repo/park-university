# Unit 1: How to Get to PuTTY and VNC Viewer at citrix.park.edu 

If you don’t have a Windows computer, you can get remote access to a Park Citrix Virtual Desktop environment, where you can run PuTTY and VNC Viewer. 

Before you can access this environment, your device will need to have the Citrix Receiver installed.   [link: https://receiver.citrix.com] Please visit from your Windows, Mac, or Linux computer to download the appropriate version for your computer. Citrix Receiver is also available from the Google Play store and the iTunes App Store for your mobile devices. You can also visit this link to find help with installing Citrix Receiver on your PC, or your mobile device.

Once installed, visit  [link: https://citrix.park.edu] Citrix.Park.EDU (from a web browser on your computer, or directly from the app on a mobile device).  Enter your ID number as your username (please note:  if you are FT Faculty, or Staff, you will enter your username instead) and enter your password, then click the option to accept the terms and conditions, and click Log On.

Some of the more simple, common applications are available on the main screen.  Click on the Desktops option at the top of the screen to launch a published desktop that has all of the available applications on it.

Click on the STUDENT option:

This will take you to a virtual published desktop with all of the applications we have available for lab/classroom use. Just like any Windows environment, double-click the application you wish to run to start it:

To open PuTTY, simply double click the PuTTY icon.

To open VNC Viewer, follow the below steps:

Click the Windows icon first.   Then click Internet Explorer  and go to  [link: https://www.realvnc.com/en/connect/download/viewer/] Download VNC Viewer

On the dropdown menu under the “Download VNC Viewer” button, select “Standalone EXE x64” and click the “Download VNC Viewer” button.

In the Pop-up bar at the bottom of the screen (as shown below), click “Run”.

Accept the agreement and click the “GOT IT” button as below.

VNC Viewer is ready to use.
