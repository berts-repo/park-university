# Unit 4: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will explore the topic of malwares. Also, we will perform a privilege escalation experiment, which will show you how the hacker controls a victim’s computer by leveraging a poor configuration on victim’s machine. You need to complete the required tasks in the below Readings and Materials section, as well as the Assignments section. You are encouraged to complete the optional and recommended tasks.”

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the behavior of different types of malware (CLO 2)

Identify and Categorize the malware based on its behaviors. (CLO 4)

Explain and Experiment with privilege escalation attack. (CLO 4)

Explain and apply countermeasures for defeating malwares. (CLO 2)
