Class,

Spam e-mails can be very difficult to remove from the email list for many reasons: the attacker can use a proxy to make it very difficult to obtain the attackers location, and spam is a easy form of profit, making it a popular threat. It is estimated that 80% of spam mail is sent via a botnet. (Goodrich & Tamassia, 509) So, in my opinion, the best solution to defeat spam would be to raise awareness and educate the average users of malware and prevent the botnets from being formed in the first place. Another possibility would be to register a email with a proof of identity, like a drivers license. Having a verified email could become a new popular standard, which would decrease the threat of spam: email providers could list all the verified emails at the top and educate the user about clicking links to non registered emails.
