# Unit 8: Overview

Introduction

In this unit, you will briefly look at the security design of different distributed applications, such as databases, email, payment systems, etc. You need to complete the required tasks in the Readings and Materials section, as well as the Assignments section. You are encouraged to complete the optional and recommended tasks.

Unit Learning Outcomes

At the conclusion of the unit, the learner will be able to:

Explain the security mechanisms in the database system. (CLO 2).

Explain the security mechanisms for email systems. (CLO 2)

Explain the security mechanisms for credit card system. (CLO 2)

Explain the security design for Digital Right Management. (CLO 2)
